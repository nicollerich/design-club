What is Design Club?

Every Friday's at 11:00AM (EST) @ BuzzFeed


Design Club is an opportunity to bring in outside voices, and hear stories from other designers with varied work and career paths. It’s open internally to everyone, and we provide a live stream for remote employees. It’s a pretty informal, intimate gathering — a good environment for questions and conversation.

Want to speak?

Or know someone who awesome who would like to speak?


We're always looking for interesting and inspirational individuals who'd like to share their process or experiences with others like them. If you're interested in speaking at Design Club please feel free to email us, with a link to your work, to schedule a Friday for you. If you know of someone else who is interested in speaking, send an intro email to one of us and we'll take it from there!

Where is this magical event?


This event is held at BuzzFeed's Headquarters in New York City. For those who are remote, you're in luck! We BlueJeans this event every Friday's @ 11am EST!

BuzzFeed Design Club is an exclusive event for BuzzFeed employees, otherwise invited by a BuzzFeed employee or by our special guests.
